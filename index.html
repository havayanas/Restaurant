Chicken Adobo


Adobo, the Spanish word for "dressing," is a spicy marinade or seasoning typically made from chili peppers, vinegar, spices, and herbs.

Pork Sinigang


Pork Sinigang is the ultimate comfort food! Made with pork ribs, vegetables, and tamarind-flavored broth, it's hearty and delicious on its own or served with steamed rice.

Sizzling Sisig


Sisig is a Filipino dish made from pork jowl and ears, pork belly, and chicken liver, which is usually seasoned with calamansi, onions, and chili peppers.

Lechon Paksiw


Lechon Paksiw made with chopped roast pork stewed in vinegar, liver sauce, and spices is perfectly sweet and tangy. It's a mouthwatering dish you'll love with steamed rice; a great use of roast pork leftovers!
